Bitter Gourd (Ampalaya)

Scientific Name: Momordica Charantia

Plant Description

Bitter gourd, also known as bitter melon or Momordica charantia, is a unique vegetable that is popular in various cuisines, particularly in Asian countries. It is characterized by its distinctive bumpy and elongated shape, and it typically has a green or yellowish-green color.

Edible Parts

Leaves, fruit, roots, stems. Both wild and cultivated forms are edible.

How to Use

You can steam, boil, stir-fry, braise, pickle, stuff, or curry bitter gourd for a variety of nutritious and delicious dishes.
